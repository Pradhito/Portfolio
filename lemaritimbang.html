Lemari Timbang

The readily available supply of raw materials to the integrated bread industry warehouse with electronic monitoring system is needed for efficiency and easier monitoring of availability of goods. In the monitoring system of goods, this cupboard has equipped with load cells sensor to detect availability of raw materials in weight (kilograms) and in weights that have been distributed to the number of units. Results from reading the load cells sensor will be processed by arduino mega 2560 with IC Wi-Fi ESP8266. Then the result are shown on the LCD 20 x 4 which is attached to the rack. Additionally, the results will also be sent via a Wi-Fi network that can be monitored throught blynk applications on a android smartphone device. When there is insufficient supply of raw material, the system will alert the smartphone to inform its employees to provide raw material immediaately.
